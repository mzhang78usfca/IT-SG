Increasing Market

How the IT market and IT labor market changed under Covid Inflation.

Visualization 1: Labor Market Change Since 2017

Here we are comparing the number of Work Permits given by the Singapore government versus the number of S passes given by the Singapore government. S passes are for mid (or above) level skilled workers and are more related to the IT industry compared with work permits. As the result, S passes didn’t decrease as dramatically as work permit during the Covid-19 pandemic, and also closely follows the size of the IT industry (see below).

Visualization 2: Overall Economy Growth V.S. IT Industry Growth

Although GDP falled down in 2020, the info-comm industry didn’t seem to be affected as much in Singapore.

How data was collected:

Work visa data are collected from Singaporean Ministry of Manpower.

GDP info are collected from The World Bank website.

InfoComm Industry Revenue data are collected from Singapore IMDA annual report.
